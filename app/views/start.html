Use this service to tell us you intend to file your overdue accounts or confirmation statement, and you want your company to remain on the register.

This will stop us from closing the company if we think it is not required because the company has not filed these documents by the filing deadline. If it is no longer required, you should close the company.

If the company's accounts are overdue, it will receive a late filing penalty that increases until it files. If the company's confirmation statement is overdue, it will not receive a penalty.
